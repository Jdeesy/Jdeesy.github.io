About Me

Career

My career journey encompasses a range of skills and focuses, you could say I'm a jack-of-all-trades. My motivation has always been about making people happy. I want to create something that people will enjoy using.

Recently I have become a Full-Stack Developer after completing Dev Bootcamp. At Dev Bootcamp, I learned many new skills such as Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and RSpec. More importantly, I learned how to pick up new technologies in a short amount of time. This new skill we allow me to grow as a developer and in all aspects of life.

Why I changed careers?

While my work has always been focused on helping people and making the community a happier place, I knew I wanted to do more to give back. This is where developing comes in. The knowledge I have gained from developing not only allows me to create meaningful things, but it also allows me to view situations or idea from a new perspective. I can bring this knowledge that I gained and improve designs.
